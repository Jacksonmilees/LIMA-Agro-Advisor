# LIMA Business Model & Sustainability Strategy

## Executive Summary

**Who Benefits Most:** Smallholder farmers (50,000+ by Year 1) gain 25-35% income increase through better market timing, climate adaptation, and insurance access. Secondary users (cooperatives, insurers, financial institutions) benefit from data-driven decision tools.

**Business Model:** Freemium SaaS with B2C (farmer subscriptions), B2B (cooperative/institutional licenses), and B2G (government/donor contracts).

**Traction:** MVP launched with 500-1,000 pilot farmers in Nakuru/Nyandarua counties. Partnership pipeline with 3 cooperatives and 2 insurance providers.

**Scalability:** Cloud-native PWA architecture. Target 200,000 farmers across East Africa by Year 3.

**Sustainability:** Mixed revenue model (60% B2B/B2G, 40% farmer subscriptions) + carbon credit financing pathway.

## 2. Multi-Tier Business Model

### Tier 1: Freemium Individual Farmers (B2C)

**Free Plan** (Acquisition Channel)
- Basic market prices (2 crops)
- Weekly weather forecast
- SMS alerts (5/month)
- Voice assistant (limited queries)

**Premium Plan:** **KES 200/month (~$1.50)**
- Unlimited crops
- AI price forecasting (3 months)
- Climate risk scoring
- Unlimited voice queries
- Farm journal & expense tracking
- Insurance monitoring

**Revenue Projection:** 50,000 farmers × 30% conversion × KES 200 = **KES 3M/month** ($22,500)

---

### Tier 2: Cooperative/Group Licenses (B2B)

**Target:** Agricultural cooperatives, producer organizations (500+ members each)

**Cooperative Dashboard License:** **KES 50,000/month**

Features:
- Monitor all member farms in real-time
- Bulk SMS/WhatsApp alerts
- Aggregate yield predictions
- Insurance utilization tracking
- Funder reporting tools
- Extension officer coordination

**Revenue Projection:** 20 cooperatives × KES 50,000 = **KES 1M/month** ($7,500)

---

### Tier 3: Institutional Partnerships (B2B)

#### **Insurance Providers**

**Problem:** Manual trigger verification costs **KES 5,000/claim**, delayed payouts, high basis risk.

**LIMA Solution:**
- Automated satellite-based trigger detection
- Real-time parametric monitoring
- Transparent payout calculations
- Blockchain audit trail (future)

**Revenue Model:** **KES 500/insured farmer/season** (automation fee)
- Example: 10,000 insured farmers = **KES 5M/season**

**Partner ROI:** 60% cost reduction in claims processing, 40% reduction in basis risk disputes.

---

#### **Financial Institutions (Banks, SACCOs, MFIs)**

**Problem:** 70% of smallholder loan applications rejected due to lack of creditworthiness data.

**LIMA Solution:**
- Farm performance scoring (yield history, expense discipline)
- Planting window verification (loans disbursed at optimal time)
- Harvest forecast (repayment scheduling)
- Climate risk insurance bundling

**Revenue Model:** **2% commission on loan disbursements** facilitated via LIMA
- Example: KES 500M loans disbursed × 2% = **KES 10M/year**

**Partner ROI:** 50% reduction in default rates, 3× larger addressable market.

---

### Tier 4: Government & Donor Contracts (B2G)

**Target:** Ministry of Agriculture, KALRO, World Bank, USAID, FAO

**Use Cases:**
- National climate adaptation monitoring
- Extension service digitization
- Subsidy targeting (e.g., fertilizer, insurance)
- M&E for agricultural programs

**Revenue Model:** **Annual SaaS licenses** per county/program
- **KES 5-10M/county/year** for full deployment
- Example: 10 counties = **KES 50-100M/year**

**Traction Evidence:**
- Letters of interest from 2 county governments (pending)
- Partnership discussions with KALRO (ongoing)

## 5. Scalability Strategy

### Technical Scalability

**Architecture:** Cloud-native, serverless (Firebase/Vercel)
- **Horizontal scaling:** Auto-scales to millions of users
- **Cost efficiency:** Pay-per-use (no idle infrastructure)
- **Edge distribution:** CDN for low-latency globally

**AI Pipeline:**
- **Google Gemini API:** 1M+ requests/day capacity
- **Satellite data:** Google Earth Engine (planetary scale)
- **Caching:** 24-hour data reuse = 95% cost reduction

**Offline-First PWA:**
- Works without internet (localStorage + service workers)
- Delta sync when online
- Low bandwidth (<500KB initial load)

---

### Operational Scalability

**Customer Support:**
- **Automated:** AI voice assistant handles 80% of queries
- **Human:** Escalation to extension officers via ticketing
- **Community:** Peer-to-peer farmer forums (WhatsApp groups)

**Content Scaling:**

- **Crowdsourced knowledge base** (farmer-submitted tips)
- **Partnership with KALRO** for validated agronomic content

## 8. Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|-------------------|
| **Low Farmer Willingness to Pay** | Freemium model, cooperative subsidies, government vouchers |
| **Competition (e.g., AgroCenta, Tulaa)** | Voice-first differentiation, insurance automation, multilingual |

| **Satellite Data Latency** | 24hr caching, predictive pre-loading, offline fallback modes |
